The Ultimate Burger Lister
Whenever a user submits a burger's name, the app will display the burger on the left side of the page -- waiting to be eaten. Each burger in the waiting area also has an Eat it now! button. When the user clicks it, the burger will move to the right side of the page.

This app stores every burger in a database, whether eaten or not. You also have the ability to remove a burger from the list at any time too.
